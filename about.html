About Art Adventures

Art Adventures is more than just an art class, it’s a creative hub! Featuring a small retail store and gallery, we showcase a variety of art supplies and handpicked creations from local and island-wide artists. Our specialty lies in affordable painting and needle felting supplies, perfect for new and aspiring artists ready to explore their creativity.

Meet Art Teacher Alicia Lumb

Founder of Art Adventures, is a classically trained artist with a rich background in animation, film, and video game artistry. With training from Emily Carr University, North Island College, and the Art Institute of Vancouver, Alicia brings over a decade of experience as an environment painter and an educator. She is passionate about empowering students to create artwork they can feel proud of—guiding even beginners to paint pieces like Van Gogh's skyline. Alicia's teaching experience spans children, teens, and adults, combining her knowledge of art with her certifications in First Aid and childhood developmental psychology. She has a heart for community service, volunteering her time for art therapy with individuals with disabilities, dementia patients, and the elderly. Whether you're picking up a brush for the first time or looking to refine your skills, Alicia’s classes provide a supportive, inspiring environment for all.
